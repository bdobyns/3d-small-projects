# Summary

This is my second work, I have obtained the CNC drawings of the following page(https://www.inventables.com/),I have processed them with Cinema 4d to give volume and relocate them, I have scaled them to be able to print them on a base of 200 mm, they have a thickness of 1.6 mm.
The result is a dinosaur of about 290 mm x 170 mm.
Enjoy
