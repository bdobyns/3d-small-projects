# Summary

A Star-Card 3D printed Christmas card. Flat (only1,5mm) for easy postage, the star model can be snapped from the supportive frame and then assembled.

I designed this for Christmas 2019. I printed it with PLA

You may need to fiddle with the settings of your printer to make sure the final print fits together well. As you can imagine the tolerances are pretty tiny.

There are two versions of this card, english and german.
